Key Features

The best task management app for the detail-oriented.

Dependent Tasks
Only see tasks you can do right now - even if you have complex dependencies.
Recurring Tasks
Set tasks to recur based on the last time you finished them. Choose to hide recurring tasks until they’re due again, or create infinitely recurring tasks like “Read for 30 minutes.”
Quick Capture
Text or type quick thoughts and sort them later, so you never forget.
Routines
Optimize the flow of starting your day, ending your day, weekly reviews, or your skincare routine.
Dashboard
Work on your top tasks from one place.
Random
Add some fun to your flow. A “random” button lets you select a random task from every list.
